Curriculum Overview

The program consists of 120 ECTS credits over two years, balancing theory and hands-on research.

  • Core Courses (41 ECTS) – Neuroscience, AI, Machine Learning, Cybernetics.
  • Elective Courses (19 ECTS) – Specialization in neuroinformatics, cognitive UX, or big data neuroscience.
  • Master Thesis (60 ECTS) – Research project with faculty or external partners.
