      <ul style="list-style: disc; margin-left: 40px;">

        <li><strong>Bachelor’s Degree:</strong> Applicants must hold a Bachelor’s (or equivalent) degree in

          See the official list of eligible degrees on the program site.</li>

        <li><strong>Transcripts:</strong> Official transcripts from all post-secondary institutions attended 

          (translated into English, German, or French if applicable).</li>

        <li><strong>Motivation Letter:</strong> A concise essay (1–2 pages) outlining your academic goals, 

          research interests, and reasons for pursuing this MSc.</li>

        <li><strong>Letters of Recommendation:</strong> 1–2 letters from professors or professionals who 

          can attest to your academic ability and potential.</li>

        <li><strong>Language Proficiency:</strong> Proof of English proficiency (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL) 

          if English is not your native language.</li>

      </ul>

      <p>

        In certain cases, applicants may be required to fulfill additional prerequisites or undertake 

        supplementary coursework. The admissions committee reviews each application holistically, 

        considering academic merit, motivation, and alignment with the program’s objectives.

      </p>
